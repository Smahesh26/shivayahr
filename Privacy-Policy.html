Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Shivaya HR, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our website and recruitment services.

Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, resume, job history, and other details submitted through job applications or inquiries.
  • Business Information: Company name, hiring requirements, and other recruitment-related data provided by employers.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site (collected via cookies).
How We Use Your Information
  • Process job applications and match candidates with suitable roles.
  • Assist employers in their hiring process.
  • Contact candidates and clients regarding job opportunities and recruitment services.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.
How We Protect Your Information
  • We secure personal data against unauthorized access or misuse.
  • Restrict access to information only to authorized personnel.
  • Use encryption and secure servers to store sensitive data.
Sharing of Information
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • We may share information with potential employers (only with candidate consent).
  • Third-party service providers for website analytics and security.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
Your Rights & Choices
  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your personal details.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and contractual obligations).
Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance website performance and user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

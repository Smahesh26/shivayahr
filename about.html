About Shivaya HR

Your trusted partner in IT, Non-IT, Technical, and Non-Technical hiring.

Who we are

At Shivaya HR, we are more than just a recruitment agency—we are your strategic hiring partner. With a deep understanding of industry-specific talent needs, we connect businesses with top-tier professionals across IT, Non-IT, Technical, and Non-Technical roles.

Our expertise lies in customized recruitment solutions, ensuring each hire aligns perfectly with your company's culture, goals, and business requirements. Whether you are a startup, SME, or a large enterprise, our expert hiring consultants help you find the right talent, the first time.

VISION

"To be the most trusted and result-driven recruitment partner for businesses worldwide."

MISSION

"To bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by providing tailored recruitment solutions that drive business success."

Why Choose Shivaya HR?

  • Industry-Specific Expertise – We understand the nuances of IT, Non-IT, Technical, and Non-Technical hiring.
  • Customized Hiring Solutions – Every business has unique needs, and we design hiring strategies to meet them.
  • No BPO Hiring – Our focus is on corporate and technical roles, ensuring high-quality placements.
  • Efficient & Fast Recruitment – Our streamlined process helps businesses hire faster without compromising quality.
  • Extensive Talent Network – We have a vast database of pre-screened, highly qualified candidates.
  • Long-Term Partnerships – We believe in building strong relationships with our clients to help them grow.
